How It Works

Five modules. One lifecycle.
Zero manual handoffs.

01

Contract Management

Enso pulls contracts from Salesforce, HubSpot, or DocuSign PDFs. AI parses every commercial term — pricing model, SKUs, billing frequency, tiering, amendments. Codified once. Executed automatically for the full contract term.

Replaces: Manual contract entry, billing spec documents, "check with the sales team" workflows
02

Usage Metering

Every usage signal — API calls, SMS, transactions, seats, campaigns — normalized and aggregated from every source. APIs, CSVs, third-party portals. Multi-entity rollups. Parent-child hierarchies. One accurate number per customer, per period.

Replaces: Vendor portal logins, Excel aggregation, data analyst requests
03

Automated Invoicing

One click raises the invoice. Enso generates it, hits the GST portal, affixes the DSC, pushes journal and revenue recognition entries to NetSuite, and emails the customer. Under a second. Configurable approval layers. Bulk actions at scale.

Replaces: 500 manual invoices a month, cross-team approvals, late invoice cycles
04

Revenue Recognition

Proportionate, milestone, or immediate — set per SKU, automated per contract. Unbilled revenue tracked at draft stage, auto-reversed on invoicing. Credit notes handled. Every entry deterministic, auditable, explainable.

Replaces: Month-end Excel rev rec workbooks, manual NetSuite entries, unbilled accrual tracking
05

Collections & DSO Management

Automated reminders that escalate by severity. Aging reports by customer, entity, account manager. DSO tracked in real time. Payment entries sync with NetSuite. Outstanding pushed to Salesforce so AMs see it without logging into finance systems.

Replaces: Collections inboxes, manual follow-up sequences, D+10 AR reporting
